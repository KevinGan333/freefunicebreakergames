🤔 Would You Rather

The ultimate conversation starter — present two fun dilemmas and let the group debate their choices. Perfect for parties, remote team socials, and classroom brain breaks.

Best forParties, Remote Teams, Classrooms
Players2–30+
Time5–15 min
FormatVerbal / Poll
DifficultyEasy

How to Play

Would You Rather is a classic conversation game that presents players with two options and asks them to choose one. The choices are often silly, thought-provoking, or surprisingly difficult — which is exactly what makes the game so fun. It sparks debates, reveals personalities, and gets everyone laughing.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Read a "Would You Rather" question aloud (or use the generator above).
  2. Each player picks their answer — no maybes allowed!
  3. Ask a few players to explain why they chose their answer.
  4. Move to the next question. Repeat as long as the group is having fun.

Tips for Hosts

  • Pick questions that match your group — keep it workplace-appropriate for meetings.
  • Encourage players to defend their choices. The "why" is where the fun lives.
  • Use a show of hands or a quick poll for larger groups.
  • Mix silly questions with more thoughtful ones to keep the energy varied.

Best Use Cases

  • Parties: The ultimate party game — easy to explain and always entertaining.
  • Remote teams: Use during virtual happy hours or team socials.
  • Classrooms: A fun brain break that gets students talking and thinking.
  • Road trips: No materials needed — just your voice and imagination.
