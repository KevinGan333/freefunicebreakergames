New Hire Icebreaker Questions for Onboarding

120+ safe, low-pressure questions for first-day onboarding, first-week check-ins, remote teams, buddy programs, and manager one-on-ones. Every question is optional — skip any that do not feel comfortable.

🎲 Random New Hire Question

Click the button below to get a question.

👋 First Day Questions

  1. What is one thing you are looking forward to in this role?
  2. What is one tool or resource you have already found helpful?
  3. What is one question on your mind after your first day?
  4. What is one thing that surprised you today in a good way?
  5. What is one thing you want to learn more about tomorrow?
  6. How would you describe your first day in one word?
  7. What is one thing that made you feel welcomed today?
  8. What is the most interesting thing you learned today?
  9. What is one thing you are still figuring out?
  10. What is one small win from your first day?
  11. What is one thing you noticed about how the team communicates?
  12. What is one thing you are curious about after meeting the team?
  13. What is one thing that feels familiar about this workplace?
  14. What is one thing you wish you had known before your first day?
  15. What is one thing you are excited to try this week?

📅 First Week Questions

  1. What is one tool you want to understand better?
  2. What is one small thing that helped you this week?
  3. What is one thing you figured out on your own?
  4. What is one question you are still holding?
  5. What is one thing about your role that is becoming clearer?
  6. What is one thing that still feels unclear?
  7. What is one person who has been helpful this week?
  8. What is one thing you want to accomplish by the end of the month?
  9. What is one thing you learned about a teammate this week?
  10. What is one thing you would change about your onboarding so far?
  11. What is one thing you have done that you are proud of this week?
  12. What is the most useful thing you learned this week?
  13. What is one thing you are looking forward to next week?
  14. What is one thing you want your manager to know?
  15. What is one thing that feels different from what you expected?

💻 Remote New Hire Questions

  1. What is your home workspace setup like?
  2. What is one thing that helps you stay focused when working remotely?
  3. What is one thing you miss about working in person?
  4. What is one thing you enjoy about working remotely?
  5. What is one communication channel you find most useful so far?
  6. What is one thing that would make remote onboarding easier for you?
  7. How do you prefer to receive feedback or answers to questions?
  8. What is one thing you do to separate work and home life?
  9. What is one way the team can help you feel more connected?
  10. What is one thing about remote work that surprised you?
  11. What is your preferred way to check in during the day?
  12. What is one thing that helps you feel part of the team?
  13. What is one thing you want to ask about remote work culture?
  14. What is one tip you would share with future remote new hires?
  15. What is one thing that made you smile in a virtual meeting?

🤝 Buddy Program Questions

  1. What is one thing your buddy has helped you understand?
  2. What is one question you felt comfortable asking your buddy?
  3. What is one thing you want to ask your buddy but have not yet?
  4. What is one thing about the team your buddy explained well?
  5. What is one thing you and your buddy have in common?
  6. What is one thing you appreciate about having a buddy?
  7. What is one thing you wish your buddy knew about your first week?
  8. What is one thing your buddy could help you with next week?
  9. What is one thing that surprised you about the buddy program?
  10. What is one tip you would give to a new buddy?
  11. What is one thing you learned from your buddy that was not in the handbook?
  12. What is one thing you want to thank your buddy for?

💼 Role and Team Questions

  1. What is one thing about your role that excites you?
  2. What is one strength you hope to bring to this team?
  3. What is one thing you want to learn from a teammate?
  4. What is one thing about the team culture you have noticed?
  5. What is one thing you wish everyone knew about your role?
  6. What is one collaboration style you prefer?
  7. What is one thing you want to contribute in your first month?
  8. What is one skill you want to build in this role?
  9. What is one thing about the team's work that impressed you?
  10. What is one thing you are still discovering about your role?
  11. What is one way you like to receive help or support?
  12. What is one thing that makes you feel like part of the team?
  13. What is one way you prefer to communicate with teammates?
  14. What is one thing you have already contributed that you are proud of?
  15. What is one goal you have for your first three months?

  3. What is one thing you are grateful for today?
  4. What is one thing that feels comfortable about this workplace?

  6. What is one thing you are still getting used to?
  7. What is one thing you would like more of in your onboarding?
  8. What is one thing that helped you feel less nervous?
  9. What is one thing you appreciate about how the team communicates?
  10. What is one small moment that stood out to you this week?
  11. What is one thing you want to try differently next week?
  12. What is one thing you hope stays the same about this team?
