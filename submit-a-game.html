Submit a Game

We love hearing about new icebreaker games! A community submission feature is planned for a future version of this website.

What to Expect

In a future update, this page will include a form where you can share your favorite icebreaker game with our community. We are looking for games that are:

  • Easy to explain in under a minute
  • Playable without special materials or apps
  • Inclusive and appropriate for group settings
  • Free to play and share

Local Game Submission Helper

This local helper does not submit your information anywhere. It only helps you draft and copy a game idea. Nothing is saved, sent, or stored.

In the Meantime

While we build the community submission feature, you are welcome to browse and play all the games currently available on the site. Every game is free, requires no signup, and works in your browser.
