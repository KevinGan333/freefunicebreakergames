Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Free Fun Icebreaker Games is designed to be a simple, privacy-respecting website. We believe that playing an icebreaker game should not require sharing personal information, and we have built this site to reflect that belief.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No login required: You do not need to create an account or sign in to use this website.
  • No personal data collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personal information.
  • No analytics: This first version of the site does not use any analytics services or tracking scripts.
  • No advertising cookies: This site does not display advertisements and does not use advertising cookies.
  • No data storage: The interactive games on this site run entirely in your browser. No game data is saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Embedded Games

All embedded games are hosted locally on this site. No third-party content is embedded, and no data is sent to external services when you play a game.

Sharing and User Responses

This site provides optional copy-to-clipboard and native browser share buttons on game pages and embedded game tools. These features work locally in your browser:

  • User responses typed into game tools are not saved by the website.
  • User responses are not sent to any server.
  • Users choose what to copy or share — nothing is posted automatically.
  • The website does not automatically post to social media platforms.
  • The website does not use social media SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party share buttons.

Local Storage

This site does not use cookies, local storage, or session storage in its current version.

Third-Party Links

This site does not contain links to third-party websites in its current version. All navigation is internal to the site.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in future versions of the site, the updated policy will be posted here. Since no personal data is collected in this version, there is nothing to retroactively apply a new policy to.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please refer to the contact information that will be provided when the site is publicly launched.
